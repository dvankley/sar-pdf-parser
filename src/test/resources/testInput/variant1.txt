               Federal Pell  Grant                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                         
               A Federal  Pell  Grant  is awarded  to undergraduate  students  who  have  financial  need  and who  have  not earned  a                  
               degree  or are in  a teacher  certification  program.  Federal  Pell Grants  don't need  to  be repaid.                                   
                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                         
               Up to $10,000                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                         
               Federal Direct  Loans                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                         
               A federal direct loan is  money lent  by  the  government  to you  that  you must  repay  with interest.                                  
                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                         
               Up to $5,000                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                         
               Federal Work-Study                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                         
               Federal Work-Study   is a  way  for  students  to earn  money  to  pay for  school  through  part-time  jobs  on or  off campus.
